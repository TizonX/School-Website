About Us


Patna Public School Danapur Cantt started functioning wef 02 Apr 93 under the aegis of AWES, New Delhi. It is located in Danapur Cantt at a distance of 12 KM. from Patna. The Commandant, Bihar Regimental Centre is the Chairman of the School Management Comittee which monitors the administration of the school.





Our Mission


Patna Public School, Danapur Cantt. strives for academic excellence and holistic development of a child and to of a child and to help them to bloom to their full potential. Our endeavor is to provide Quality Education at Affordable Cost. We aim at instilling in our children a strong sense of values, discipline and character groom them to become responsible citizens. We firmly believe that a learning community exists only when both the facilitator and the recipient simultaneously learn from each other.
